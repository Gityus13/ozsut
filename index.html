Our Story

Bringing the Taste of Azerbaijan & Turkey to Your Table

Founded in 2015, OZSUT is a culinary bridge between the rich traditions of Azerbaijani and Turkish cuisine. Our name, meaning "unique taste" in Azerbaijani, reflects our commitment to delivering authentic flavors with a modern twist.

Our chefs, trained in both culinary traditions, use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create dishes that tell the story of these two great cultures.
